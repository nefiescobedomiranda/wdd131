Purpose

This educational platform provides information about mining, mineral processing plants, equipment, major mining operations, and professional careers related to the mining industry.

Career Opportunities

Mining Engineer

Plans and supervises ore extraction activities.

Work Areas: Open-pit mines, underground mines, mine planning departments.

Metallurgical Engineer

Optimizes mineral processing and recovery.

Work Areas: Crushing, grinding, flotation, thickening and filtration circuits.

Geological Engineer

Studies mineral deposits and evaluates resources.

Work Areas: Exploration projects and geological departments.

Process Plant Operator

Operates and monitors concentrator plant equipment.

Work Areas: Crushers, mills, flotation cells and filters.

Maintenance Engineer

Maintains mining and processing equipment.

Work Areas: Mechanical and electrical maintenance departments.

Environmental Engineer

Ensures environmental compliance and sustainability.

Work Areas: Tailings management and environmental monitoring.
