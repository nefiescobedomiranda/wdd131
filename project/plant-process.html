Mineral Processing Plant

Industrial process that transforms raw ore into valuable concentrates.

Process Stages

Crushing

Reduces ore size using jaw and cone crushers.

Grinding

Ball mills grind ore into fine particles.

Flotation

Separates valuable minerals using reagents and bubbles.

Thickening

Removes water from concentrate.

Equipment

  • Jaw Crusher
  • Ball Mill
  • Flotation Cell
  • Thickener
