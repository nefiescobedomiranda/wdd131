Mineral Processing Plant

Mineral processing involves transforming raw ore into valuable concentrates through mechanical and chemical processes.

Processing Stages

1. Crushing

Large rocks are reduced in size using jaw and cone crushers.

2. Grinding

Ball mills grind ore into fine particles to liberate minerals.

3. Flotation

Separation of valuable minerals using chemical reagents and bubbles.

4. Thickening

Removes water from concentrate to produce final product.

Key Equipment

  • Jaw Crusher – Primary size reduction
  • Ball Mill – Fine grinding of ore
  • Flotation Cell – Mineral separation
  • Thickener – Solid-liquid separation
